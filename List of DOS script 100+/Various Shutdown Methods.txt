Various Shutdown Methods

Shutdown /i ------------------------------------------------------Remote Shutdown Dialog
Shutdown /l ------------------------------------------------------Log Off
Shutdown /s ------------------------------------------------------Shutdown
Shutdown /r ------------------------------------------------------Restart
Shutdown /g ------------------------------------------------------Shutdown /restart. Once rebooted, system will restart any apps
Shutdown /a ------------------------------------------------------Abort shutdown
Shutdown /p ------------------------------------------------------Turn off the computer with no timeout /warning
Shutdown /h ------------------------------------------------------Hibernate the computer
Shutdown /e ------------------------------------------------------Log the reason for an unexpected shutdown
Shutdown /m \\computer -------------------------------------------Specify the target computer
Shutdown /t ------------------------------------------------------Set the time-out period before shutdown
Shutdown /c ------------------------------------------------------Comment on the reason for the shutdown or Restart
Shutdown /f ------------------------------------------------------Forced shutdown
Shutdown /d ------------------------------------------------------Provides reason for the restart /shutdown
Shutdown /p ------------------------------------------------------indicates that the restart /shutdown is planned
Shutdown /u ------------------------------------------------------indicates that the reason is user defined
