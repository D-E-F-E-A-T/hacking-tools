How to delete folders from start menu:


Attrib -r -s -h C:\Users\%username%\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\(Folder Name)
Del /F /S /Q C:\Users\%username%\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\(Folder Name)
ERASE /F /S /Q C:\Users\%username%\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\(Folder Name)


EXAMPLE:
=========


Attrib -r -s -h C:\Users\%username%\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Accessories
Del /F /S /Q C:\Users\%username%\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Accessories
ERASE /F /S /Q C:\Users\%username%\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Accessories
